FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about TopCandidate

How accurate is the AI scoring?
+
TopCandidate uses a 3-step pipeline — first structuring the CV, then parsing the job description, then scoring against structured data (not raw text). Every CV is scored twice and results averaged to ensure consistency. Our double-run method gives a confidence indicator so you always know how reliable the score is. In testing, scores are consistent within 5 points across runs 95% of the time.
How long does it take to screen 100 CVs?
+
Batch screening processes 5 CVs in parallel. For 100 CVs, typical time is 25–40 minutes depending on CV length and OpenAI API response times. You can watch live progress as each CV is scored in real-time — no need to wait for the full batch to complete.
Is my CV data secure and private?
+
Yes. Each company account has fully isolated data — no other company can see your candidates or screening results. CVs are stored encrypted in MongoDB Atlas. Data is never shared with third parties or used to train AI models. We comply with standard data protection practices.
What file formats are supported?
+
Currently we support PDF files only. The PDF must be text-based (not scanned images). We recommend asking candidates to submit their CVs as PDF exports from Word or Google Docs for best results. Scanned PDFs and image-based PDFs cannot be processed.
Can I use this for any type of role?
+
Yes — TopCandidate works for any role. The AI reads your job description and extracts the specific requirements, skills, and seniority level for that role. Whether you're hiring software engineers, data analysts, marketing managers, or customer service reps, the system adapts to your JD automatically.
Do I need technical knowledge to use it?
+
No technical knowledge required at all. Simply paste your job description, upload the CVs, and click screen. The AI handles everything. Results are presented in plain language with clear recommendations — Strong Hire, Hire, Maybe, or Reject — with specific reasoning for each decision.
What is the difference between Trial and Starter?
+
The Trial plan is free forever and gives you 10 screenings per month with single CV screening only — great for testing the product. The Starter plan ($29/month) gives you 100 screenings, batch screening for up to 20 CVs at once, and basic analytics. Upgrade anytime, no lock-in.
How do I get started?
+
Click "Start free — no card needed" above, create your account with your company email, and you're ready to screen candidates in under 2 minutes. No credit card required for the Trial plan. The system will guide you through your first screening.
